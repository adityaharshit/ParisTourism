About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the world's most iconic cities, known for its rich history, culture, and architecture. Often called the "City of Light" (La Ville Lumière), it has been a major center of art, fashion, and philosophy for centuries. Famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral attract millions of visitors each year. The city is also renowned for its charming streets, vibrant cafés, and culinary scene, particularly its bread, wine, and cheese. Paris sits along the Seine River, offering beautiful bridges and riverside views, contributing to its timeless charm.
