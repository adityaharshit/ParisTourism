Cuisine of Paris

One of the many things France is famous for is its cuisine. And luckily for you, you can enjoy it while in Paris. French cuisine is renowned for its elegance and diversity, emphasizing quality ingredients, rich flavors, and meticulous techniques. Famous for bread, cheese, and wine, iconic dishes include coq au vin, bouillabaisse, and escargot. French pastry, such as croissants and éclairs, also plays a central role in its culinary tradition.

Must try Foods

Baguette

The baguette is a staple of French cuisine, known for its crisp, golden crust and soft, airy interior. This long, slender loaf of bread is typically enjoyed fresh from bakeries, used for sandwiches, or served with cheese, butter, and jam. It’s an essential part of daily life in France.

Croque Monsieur & Croque Madame

Croque Monsieur is a traditional French ham and cheese sandwich, grilled and topped with béchamel sauce. Its variation, Croque Madame, includes a fried or poached egg on top. Both are popular in Parisian bistros, offering a delicious twist on a classic sandwich, perfect for brunch or lunch.

Coq au vin

Coq au Vin is a classic French dish made with chicken slow-cooked in red wine, often with mushrooms, onions, and bacon. The rich, flavorful sauce is infused with herbs and garlic. Traditionally served with mashed potatoes or crusty bread, it's a comforting and hearty meal.
